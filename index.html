How To Play

Rock Paper Scissors, first to 10 points wins (15 vs CPU). Each hand is assigned a reward for winning (1-3pts in 2 Player, 1-6pts vs CPU), and it changes each round. Should you go for the highest reward, or is your opponent one step ahead? Let the mind games begin!
